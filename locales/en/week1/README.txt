MISSION: ARRIVAL AT BLACKTHORN MANOR

Welcome, Explorer.

A letter with no return address has led you to Blackthorn Manor.

The estate is old, silent, and strangely intact.

At the center of the entrance hall stands a terminal.

The letter contains one instruction:

"Learn the language of the manor, and the manor will answer."

Your mission:

1. Discover where you are.
2. Explore the rooms of the manor.
3. Read the clues left behind.
4. Find the hidden vault.
5. Recover the Week 1 completion key.

Commands to practice:

pwd
ls
cd
cat
clear
history

Hints:

* Use pwd to discover your current location.
* Use ls to see nearby rooms and files.
* Use cd to move between rooms.
* Use cat to read text files.
* If the terminal becomes crowded, use clear.
* If you forget what you typed, use history.

Your backpack is located here:

~/terminal-explorer/backpack/

It contains:

spellbook.txt
journal.txt
achievements.txt
lore.txt
discoveries.txt

The backpack belongs to you.

The course will not manage it for you.

When you discover a new spell, modifier, piece of lore, or achievement, write it down.

Terminal Realm Terminology:

Spell      = Command
Modifier   = Flag
Target     = Argument
Ritual     = Script
Portal     = Symbolic Link
Grimoire   = Manual Page

Example:

ls -a mansion

Spell:
ls

Modifier:
-a

Target:
mansion

Meaning:

Reveal all files, including hidden ones, inside the mansion.

Optional:

Some secrets are hidden.

You do not need them to complete Week 1.

Curious explorers may discover hidden rooms, hidden notes, and forgotten paths.

A curious explorer may eventually learn:

ls -a

Advanced spells may be studied through the Grimoire:

man <spell>

Example:

man ls

The Grimoire contains ancient knowledge about every spell.

Arabic-speaking students:

Some terminals do not display Arabic text correctly.

If a file is difficult to read in the terminal, open it in VS Code:

code README.txt
